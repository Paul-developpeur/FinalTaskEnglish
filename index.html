Toy of The Year (TOTY) 2020

Hulk with the problem of diabetes

Here is this year's new toy!

Your favourite hero is now in possession of diabetes but he can still fight the bad guys.


If you're like that, help the Hulk fight the bad guys with him so he doesn't suffer any more.


Protect your city even with a disability, don't affect your sense of justice.


This toy was created to include children suffering from diabetes to make them understand that even with a disability you can remain strong and be accepted.

To purchase this toy, click on 'add'.

By doing this action you save several children who are suffering and struggling to be accepted.
